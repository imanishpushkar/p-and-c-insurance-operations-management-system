# P&C Insurance Operations Management System

## Run Your Entire Property & Casualty Insurance Business From One Modern Platform

**P&C Insurance Operations Management System** is a comprehensive **PHP & MySQL insurance CRM** designed for insurance companies, brokers, agencies, underwriters, claims teams, surveyors, and third-party administrators.

Manage policyholders, brokers, insurance products, underwriting, policies, claims, inspections, fraud investigations, settlements, compliance, renewals, documents, and reports from one modern dashboard.

Built as a **self-hosted solution** with a **one-time purchase** and **lifetime ownership**, the platform gives complete control over your insurance operations without recurring subscription fees.

Whether you manage motor, property, travel, commercial, liability, marine, or other general insurance products, this system provides everything needed to operate a modern insurance business.
